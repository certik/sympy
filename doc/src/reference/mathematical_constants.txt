Mathematical Constants
======================

http://reference.wolfram.com/mathematica/guide/MathematicalConstants.html

SymPy incorporates the latest algorithms for evaluating mathematical constants
to any number of digits of precision. For basic constants like pi and e,
millions of digits can routinely be computed in seconds.
